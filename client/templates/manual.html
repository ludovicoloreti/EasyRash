Introduction

EasyRash is a project creted by Filippo Boiani and Ludovico Loreti with the aim to help researchers to comment and share articles in an easy and simple way.

The article should be written in RASH, a framework which includes a markup language defined as a subset of HTML+RDF for writing scientific articles, and related tools to convert it into different formats, to extract data from it, etc.

Actions

Annotator mode

When you click on this button you enter the annotator mode which allows you to create selections, underline text, create comments and vote the article.

Only one person is allowed to be in annotator mode for each document. When you click on the button you acquire a lock. If someone has acquired the lock before you should wait until it has finished.

When you have finished you can either save you comments or leave without saving by clicking on

Vote article

When you are in annotator mode, you can give a general vote to the entire article. The vote is a number between 1 and 5.

The chair decides either to accept the article or reject the article on the average vote express by all reviewers.

Once you gave a vote to the article you can't express the vote another time.

Show comment list

By clicking on this button you will see a modal containing a table that lists all the comment you have written in annotator mode and that are not saved.

You can update, delete and insert comments as you like.

Create note

After a selection you can press this button in order to highlight the selected text. By highlighting you will be able to comment the fragment.

Overlapping fragment are not allowd, so you should verify what other users have commented.

Exit annotator mode

When you push this button you exit the annotator mode and al your comments will be saved or deleted.
